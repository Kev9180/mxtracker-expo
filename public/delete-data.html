Request Deletion of Specific Data (Without Deleting Account)

Last updated: March 24, 2026

MXTracker allows users to request deletion of some or all account data while keeping the account active.

How to request specific data deletion

  1. Send an email to developer@mxtracker.app.
  2. Use subject MXTracker Data Deletion Request.
  3. Include the email used for your MXTracker account.
  4. Specify what to delete (for example: one vehicle, all maintenance records, reminders, push token, or all app data while keeping your login).

Verification: For security, we may ask you to verify account ownership before processing deletion.

Examples of data you can ask us to delete

  • Single vehicle and related records.
  • All reminders.
  • All maintenance history.
  • Push notification token and preference data.
  • All app data except your account login credentials.

Data retained after partial deletion

  • Your account login record remains so you can continue using MXTracker.
