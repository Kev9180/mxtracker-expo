How to Delete Your MXTracker Account

Last updated: March 24, 2026

This page explains how users of the MXTracker app can request account deletion and what data is deleted or retained.

Option 1: Delete your account in the app (fastest)

  1. Open MXTracker and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the action.

Option 2: Email request

If you cannot access the app, send an email to developer@mxtracker.app from your account email with subject MXTracker Account Deletion Request.

Verification: For security, we may ask you to verify account ownership before deleting data.

What data is deleted

  • Account profile and authentication record.
  • Garage data (vehicles and details).
  • Maintenance records and reminder configuration.
  • Push notification token and in-app preferences linked to your account.

What data may be retained temporarily

  • Supabase may retain logs or backups for a certain amount of time. Direct any questions or concerns to Supabase customer support.
